Coconut Nobake Cookies

Back to main page

Cook time = prep + 1 minute at boil

Need: medium pot, cookie sheet, and parchment paper

Ingredients

  • 3 cups oatmeal
  • 1 cup coconut
  • 2 Tbsp cocoa
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 square baking chocolate

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients.
  2. Add wet ingredients to a medium pot. Mix and then add dry ingredients. Bring to a boil.
  3. Let boil for 1 minute.
  4. Remove from heat and place cookie sized dollups onto parchment lined cookie sheet.
  5. Let cool.
