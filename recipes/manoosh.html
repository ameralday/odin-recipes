Manoosh

Back to recipes

Cook time = 7-8 minutes at 500 F

Need pizza pan or cookie sheet

Note: Dough is very wet and soft. It is best made in kitchen processor.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp dry active yeast
  • 1 tsp white sugar
  • 200 ml lukewarm water
  • 350 g flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp evoo plus extra for pan and topping
  • za'atar topping (see below)
  1. Mix 1/2 of the water, yeast, and sugar. Let sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Add the rest of hte water, salt, evoo, and sift in flour.
  3. Mix on lowest setting with dough hook for 1 minute.
  4. Lightly oil large bowl. Add dough and cover with plastic wrap. Place in warm area. Let rise and double in size for 90 minutes.
  5. Put on well floured surface and divide into 6 balls.
  6. Flatten and spread on za'atar mixture.

Za'atar topping is a mixture of about 125ml evoo and 3 Tbsp za'atar seasoning.
