Jollof Rice

Spicy and Delicious

Cook time = 45 minutes.

Needs: medium pot with lid, foil

Ingredients

  • 1 can fire roasted tomatoes or 4 fresh
  • 1 bell pepper
  • i red onion
  • 1 thumb ginger
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 hot pepper (scotch bonnet, habenero)
  • 1/4 cup sunflower oil
  • 1 cup long grain rice (washed)
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  1. Blend tomato, peppers, onion, and ginger, in food processor to make paste
  2. In large pot over medium heat, Add oil and mixture/paste. Stir and allow to sizzle. Place lid, reduce heat to low, and simmer for 20 minutes.
  3. Add spices and seasoning. Stir.
  4. Place foil into pot covering the rice.
  5. Add lid and cook for 20 minutes over low heat.
  6. Remove foil, return lid, cook for 5 more minutes.
