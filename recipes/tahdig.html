Crispy Persian Rice

Back to main page

Cook time = 1 1/2 hours

Need: large pot with lid

  • 1 tsp saffron threads
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1-2 Tbsp kosher salt
  • 2 Tbsp whole milk yogurt (greek)
  • 2 Tbsp oil
  • 1 cup dried cherries
  • grated zest of 1 orange
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 4-8 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 3 Tbsp pistachios
  1. Mix saffron into 1 cup very warm water. Let sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Rinse rice under cool running water.
  3. In large pot, combine 8 cups water, rice and salt. Cook for 5-6 minutes and drain.
  4. In medium bowl, combine 1 cup rice, yogurt, oil, and 2 Tbsp saffron water. Mix thoroughly.
  5. Spread mixture evenly on bottom of 10 inch lidded nonstick pan. Sprinkle 1 cup rice on top, 2 Tbsp dried cherries, orange zest, and a pinch of cinnamon.
  6. Add another layer of rice and repeat reserving some cherries for garnish.
  7. Dot with butter and pour the rest of the saffron water on top.
  8. Wrap lid with kitchen towel and secure around the handle with a rubber band.
  9. Cover and cook over low heat 25-30 minutes until rice around the edges is golden and crispy. It will take about 50 minutes. You can open the lid to check.
  10. Invert a large serving plate and flip together carefully.
